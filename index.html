VIGHNANA 1.0

VIGHNANA 1.0 is an iniiative of University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun to motivate and promote youth to participate. The event will be a National level platform for budding coders and to motivate them to take up more challenging and competitive projects. The main objective of the competition is to provide a forum to showcase the working prototype and technical dares implemented in Computer Science, Information Technology and related areas.
